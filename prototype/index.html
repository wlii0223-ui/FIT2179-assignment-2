Data Visualisation 2 Prototype

Australia Under Pressure

A first pass at the core story: recent recovery activations are dominated by flood and rainfall events, while historical disasters show distinct spatial and human-impact patterns.

Modern recovery burden

DRFA activations surged in recent years

The DRFA dataset records recovery activations by local government area. This first prototype checks whether the annual pattern is strong enough to anchor the middle of the final story.

Historical footprint

Major disaster events cluster along exposed coasts and settled regions

The historical event dataset is filtered to Australian natural disasters only. Points are coloured by disaster category and sized by an impact score derived from recorded deaths, injuries, evacuations and property damage.
